Notes

RTS = Ready to Send
RTR = Ready to Receive
FIN = File Transfer Finished
NAK = Not Acknowledged
ACK = Acknowledged
TOT = Time-Out Timer


Modes:

MODE1 - Fastest & Least Robust
PWR = 6
 BW = 500
 SF = 8
 CR = 4/6

MODE2 - Middle Ground
PWR = 12
 BW = 250
 SF = 10
 CR = 4/7

MODE3 - Slowest & Most Robust
PWR = 17 (or possibly 20)
 BW = 125
 SF = 12
 CR = 4/8
